How it all started

The seed of Pangaea was initiated when it was planted by Saud Aleidi and Mohammad Almulhim, enthusiastic, adventurous travelers, as they began traveling, hiking, and mountaineering at an early age. With the passage of attempts and experiences, they became among the most famous travelers in the Kingdom of Saudi Arabia.

Pangaea took out its branches and grew to have adventure centers spread in the regions of the Kingdom and worldwide. It became famous as one of the best tourist centers, as it launched trips and exploration programs for groups and individuals according to their preferences. Pangaea offers different experiences and services, such as outdoor activities, overnight services, workshops, and travel societies.

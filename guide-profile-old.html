About Abdulrazzaq Almojalhem

Abdulrazzaq Almojalhem, a mountaineer from Kuwait, has achieved remarkable feats in the world of mountaineering. With extensive experience and a fiery spirit, he has successfully conquered four of the Seven Summits, including the impressive solo of Aconcagua in just 9 days. His name also graces Everest's summit in 2023, facing unforgettable challenges amidst extreme temperatures and the looming risks of altitude sickness

Qualifications & Certifications

  • Certified Wilderness First Responder
  • Professional Tour Guide Association Member
  • Licensed by the Saudi Commission for Tourism & National Heritage

Guiding Philosophy

"Each journey is a story waiting to unfold. As your guide, I promise to make each chapter memorable."

Areas of Expertise

  • Expert Guidance: Abdulrazzaq's extensive experience and expertise, you'll benefit from top-notch guidance throughout your journey. Whether you're a novice or an experienced climber, his insights and knowledge will enhance your understanding of mountaineering techniques, safety protocols, and expedition strategies.
  • Educational Insight: Throughout the expedition, Abdulrazzaq shares his wealth of knowledge about mountaineering, imparting valuable insights into navigation, safety procedures, and mountain ecology. You'll gain a deeper understanding of the natural world and the challenges inherent in high-altitude environments, enriching your experience beyond the physical ascent.
  • Safety and Well-being: Safety is paramount under Abdulrazzaq's supervision. He meticulously plans and prepares for every aspect of the journey, prioritizing the well-being of each team member. From monitoring weather conditions to implementing emergency response protocols, you'll feel reassured knowing that your safety is in capable hands.
